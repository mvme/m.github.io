idea设置
=========================

idea设置显示行号
------------------------

> #### 1. File>Settings>Editor>General>Appearance>show line numbers
> #### 2. view>Active Editor>Show Line Numbers

***

idea快捷键使用
------------------------

> #### 1. 自动导包：Alt+Enter
> #### 2. getter and setter: Alt+Insert
> #### 3. main方法: psvm
> #### 4. system.out: sout
